Grilled Cheese Sandwich

Best. Sandwich. Ever.

You can put almost anything in a grilled cheese sandwich and it will be delicious!

Ingredients

  • 2 slices bread, any sliced bread will work
  • sliced or shredded cheese, any melting cheese will work
  • 2 tablesppons butter, softened
  • meat, pesto, tomato or any other toppings, optional

Cooking Instructions

  1. Pre-heat frying pan over medium heat
  2. Spread butter on one side of each slice of bread
  3. Place bread butter side down on frying pan
  4. Cover bread in pan with cheese, add any additional toppings
  5. Place other buttered slice of bread on top (butter side out)
  6. Cook until golden brown and cheese is melted
