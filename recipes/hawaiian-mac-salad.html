Hawaiian Mac Salad

Easy take on a Hawaiian Favorite!

Quick and easy recipe for Hawaiian Mac Salad, often served with plate lunch style Hawaiian food. A staple for any BBQ!

Ingredients

  • 1 pound bag elbow macaroni
  • 2 eggs, hardboiled
  • 2 carrots, grated
  • 1/4 cup sweet onion, grated
  • 2 1/2 cups Mayonnaise Best Foods or Hellmans's mayonnaise (no substitutions!)
  • 1/4 cup milk
  • 2 tablespoons apple cider vinegar
  • 2 teaspooons sugar
  • salt and pepper, to taste

Instructions

  1. cook elbow macaroni per instructions on package
  2. add apple cider vinegar to noodles, along with grated carrot and onion
  3. mix well, and let cool 10 minutes
  4. in a separate bowl combine mayo, milk and sugar. Mix and add to cooled noodles
  5. add salt and pepper to taste
