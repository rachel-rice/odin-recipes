Hawaiian Mac Salad

Easy take on a Hawaiian Favorite!

Quick and easy recipe for Hawaiian Mac Salad. A staple for any BBQ!

Ingredients

  • 1 pound bag elbow macaroni
  • 2 eggs, hardboiled
  • 2 carrots, shredded
  • 1/4 cup onion, shredded (optional)
  • 2 1/2 cups Mayonnaise Best Foods or Hellmans's mayonnaise (no substitutions!)
  • 1/4 cup milk
  • 2 tablespoons apple cider vinegar
  • 2 teaspooons sugar
  • salt and pepper, to taste

Instructions

  1. cook elbow macaroni per instructions on package
  2. once macaroni is cooled, combine with all ingredients
  3. mix well
  4. add salt and pepper to taste
