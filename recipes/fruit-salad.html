Fruit Salad

Healthy and Delicious Dish!

You can use prety much any fruit to make this healthy snack or side. Cut fruit tossed in a bowl is super easy and delicious!

Ingredients

  • Strawberries, halved
  • Blueberries
  • Grapes
  • Kiwi, peeled and sliced
  • Pineapple, cubed
  • Mandarin Oranges, peeled

Instructions

  1. Clean and cut all fruit
  2. Toss into large bowl
  3. Mix and Serve!
